The role of Women during the Renaissance


Intro

The renaissance took place in the 1600’s therefore; women did not have many rights. Their main job was to do the housework around the house and take care of the children. However, the women who were able to stay home and do nothing else were only the women of classes. The “normal” also known as peasant women had to work in the field alongside their husbands and take care of the housework at home. Women who did not manage to marry someone had to stay at their male relatives houses.

Who ruled over the women?

Subjugation is a great word to describe the way everyone treated women during the renaissance. Somebody always controlled women, in the beginning of their life it was their father who controlled them and when they got married it was their husband’s job. Often women in Florence, Italy would be married away at the early age of around 13 while the husbands would be in the around his thirties. The husbands often died when the women was relatively young leaving many rich widows lonely. When the women became widows, the family would instantly pressure them to get married again so they would not be alone. How women was treated depending on their class The women of the upper class were also subjugated but in a different way. They were told not to go outside and work so they had to stay in all day. Even breastfeeding was considered a job and only women of the lower classes were allowed to breastfeed their babies. Women of lower classes lived quite differently in Italy compared to Great Britain. In Italy you could see a lot of the low class women walking around on the street talking to each other while you would not see any of the higher class women walking around with the. Church was the only place where you would see all the women being together at the same place and same time.

Feminism

Some women of the higher classes were actually fortunate enough to work as authors. Women wrote all kinds of books, such as poetry, romances, stories and novels. However, only the women of the higher classes knew how to write and read. The writings that happened during the renaissance helped start the revolution of feminism. During the renaissance, there were many feminists, especially in Italy. The feminists thought that women had the same equalities as men and deserved to be treated equally. They display these thoughts through texts and sometime through actions. One of the actions they did was dressing up in male clothing. You could say women during the Renaissance did not live a good life. Since the renaissance took place a couple of hundred years ago, it is not really a shock to anyone. To sum up the renaissance you could say the rich women had a good life with many privileges, and the poor women lived a horrible life.
